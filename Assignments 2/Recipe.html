Recipe: Vegetable Sandwich

Ingredients

The following ingredients are required:

  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green chutney

Preparation Steps

Follow these steps to prepare the sandwich:

  1. Take two bread slices and apply butter evenly.
  2. Spread green chutney on one side of the bread.
  3. Add boiled potato slices over the bread.
  4. Place tomato and onion slices on top.
  5. Sprinkle salt and pepper as required.
  6. Cover with another bread slice.
  7. Cut the sandwich and serve fresh.
